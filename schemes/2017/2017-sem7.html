How it works :

  1. Enter your each subject marks out of 100.
  2. Enter 0 if you have a backlog in the subject.
  3. Enter the new marks given by VTU after clearing the subject.
    Example : If you scored 12 in first attempt and then scored 45 then enter the new marks ie: 45.
  4. After entering marks click calculate and you will see the SGPA and Percentage displayed in the box.
  5. Click reset to clear all the fields.
