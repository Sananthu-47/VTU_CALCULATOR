Calculation for SGPA

  1. CGPA is the aggrigate value obtained by your overall credits earned in all semester.
  2. Collect the total credit points of your each semester provided by VTU.
  3. Add all the credits of the semesters you want to.
  4. Add all the SGPA marks of the semesters you have completed.
  5. Now divide the overall credits obtained by each semester to the overal credits of that semesters.
  6. The obtained value is your CGPA till your current semester.
