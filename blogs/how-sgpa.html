Calculation for SGPA

  1. SGPA is the aggrigate value obtained by your overall credits earned in 1 semester.
  2. Collect the credit points of your semester subject provided by VTU.
  3. Convert the marks obtained in 100 to a value of 10 by looking into the bellow table.


  4. Now the obtained marks will be your credits obtained for that subject in the scale of 10.
  5. Now multiply the credits obtained by each subjects to the credits given by VTU for that subject.
  6. Add all the obtained products and divide it with the total credits of you semester.
  7. The obtained value is your SGPA for your current semester.
